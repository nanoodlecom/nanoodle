## Why this ordering

- The three 30-min days get venues where a single well-written paste is the whole job.
  The two 2-h days get the Reddit launches, which live or die on first-hour replies.
- Show HN / Product Hunt stay banked for Jul 14–15: launching them on a day you can't
  sit in the comments would burn the two best cards. Post-cookoff they're stronger —
  user-built winners are the social proof — and both drafts are finished now, so the
  Fable cost gate doesn't touch them.
- HN and PH are split across two days on purpose: each wants your full attention, and
  a good HN thread the day before is quotable material in the PH launch.
- AlternativeTo is hard-gated until Jul 13 05:49 Stockholm (7-day account age), which
  happens to be the ideal slot anyway.
